# Reference Research Checklist

## Surfaces

- Landing or taste gate
- Logged-in home
- Search/filter/tag pages
- Detail pages
- Start/entry gate
- Main work surface: chat, editor, board, player, dashboard
- Side panels, drawers, settings
- Creation flows
- Result/completion flows
- Account, billing, currency, subscription
- Empty, loading, error, locked, safe-mode, and permission states

## Interaction Depth

For each important action, record:

- Entry point
- User intent
- Precondition
- Immediate feedback
- Intermediate state
- Done state
- Data created or mutated
- Cost or risk
- Screenshot evidence
- Verification status

## Mechanism Depth

For features that depend on hidden system behavior, record:

- What the product claims or implies
- What inputs appear to influence the outcome
- What output the user sees
- Whether the user can inspect or correct the outcome
- Whether the product explains why the outcome happened
- What happens when the outcome is wrong or disputed
- Whether money, ranking, safety, or creator reputation is affected

## Red Flags

- A feature exists only as a tab label but its working surface was not tested.
- A paid action is inferred without seeing the price or confirmation.
- A storyboard frame is created for a flow that was not observed or explicitly marked as assumed.
- Comments or reviews reveal confusion that the product UI does not explain.
- "AI remembers", "AI judges", "AI recommends", or "AI creates" appears without a visible user promise, control, or failure UX.
