# Visual Quality Checklist

## AI Slop Signals

- Purple/blue gradient tech palette without product reason
- Glass cards everywhere
- Generic dark dashboard with neon accents
- Decorative blobs, orbs, glow, bokeh, or meaningless abstract art
- Identical repeated cards with icon, heading, paragraph
- Overlarge hero text inside an app workflow
- Fake-looking placeholder characters or screenshots
- Screens that ignore the reference flow
- Text too small to read in the delivered board
- Design system tokens that do not appear in screen examples

## Production Design Readiness

- Named art direction with rationale
- Typography roles and scale
- Color roles tied to product states
- Layout rules for mobile and desktop
- Components tied to storyboard frames
- States for trust-critical mechanisms
- Accessibility and contrast notes
- Screenshot proof of the design board
- Explicit list of what a human designer/frontend team must still resolve

## Reference Fidelity

- Compare reference screen order to proposed screen order
- Confirm entry gates are preserved
- Confirm side panels, settings, and result states were not ignored
- Mark every unobserved behavior as assumption
- Do not make improvements indistinguishable from observed behavior
