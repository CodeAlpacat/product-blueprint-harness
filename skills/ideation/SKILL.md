# Product Blueprint Ideation

Use this when there is no strong reference product, or before committing to a product direction.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the subject: audience, job-to-be-done, emotional promise, business model, and platform.
2. Generate 3 to 5 product theses. Each thesis must have a distinct core loop, not just a different visual style.
3. Score theses by user pull, differentiation, feasibility, monetization, and risk.
4. Identify product mechanisms each thesis depends on, such as memory, judging, scoring, recommendations, matching, or creator validation.
5. Pick one recommended direction and name its MVP loop.
6. Create assumption-labeled concept screens only after the loop and mechanism assumptions are clear.
7. Define what future reference research must verify.

## Output Contract

Produce `01-ideation.md` with:

- Product theses
- Target users and anti-users
- Core loop candidates
- Differentiation map
- Risk assumptions
- Mechanism assumptions
- MVP recommendation
- Concept storyboard scope

When visualizing without references, label every screen as `Assumption` rather than `Observed`.

## Next Step
